Bugatti Bolide

Engine & Performance

Powertrain:
Naturally aspirated 8.3L V16 engine producing 1000 hp, paired with three electric motors delivering an additional 800 hp, for a combined output of approximately 1800 hp.

Rev Limit:
Up to 9,000 RPM, delivering a highly emotional and engaging sound experience.

0–62 mph (0–100 km/h):
Approximately 2.0 seconds.

Top Speed:
Around 276 mph (445 km/h) when unlocked with the Speed Key.

Electric Range:
More than 60 km (37 miles) in pure electric mode.

Design & Engineering

Chassis:
Lightweight carbon fiber monocoque chassis, engineered to be lighter than the Chiron despite the added hybrid technology.

Aerodynamics:
Advanced aerodynamic package featuring flying fenders, a large rear diffuser, and an active rear wing optimized for downforce and cooling.

Body Design:
An evolution of the Chiron’s iconic design, incorporating classic Bugatti elements such as the horseshoe grille and the signature C-line.

Interior & Technology

Gauge Cluster:
Highly detailed analog gauge cluster crafted from titanium, ruby, and sapphire, inspired by Swiss watchmaking and visible around the steering wheel.

Infotainment Screen:
Hidden pop-up display that supports Apple CarPlay, maintaining a clean and timeless interior aesthetic.

Seating:
Fixed seats with an adjustable pedal box to ensure a perfect driving position.

Materials:
Premium use of aluminum, crystal glass, carbon fiber, and fine leather throughout the cabin.

Key Innovations

Naturally Aspirated V16 Engine:
Replaces the quad-turbo W16, offering a purer, more emotional driving sound and response.

Rolling Chassis Design:
Mechanical components are designed to be both functional and visually artistic.

Hybrid Integration:
Seamless blend of electric and combustion power, enhancing performance, efficiency, and electric-only range.

Availability

Production:
Limited to just 250 units worldwide.

Price:
Starting at approximately €3.2 million (net), or around $4.6 million.

Delivery:
Customer deliveries are expected to begin in 2026.
