Bugatti Chiron

Record & Production

Record-Breaking Feat:
The original prototype became the first production-based car to exceed 300 mph, achieving a top speed of 490.484 km/h.

Limited Production:
Only 30 units of the Chiron Super Sport 300+ were produced exclusively for customers.

Engine & Performance

Engine Power:
Quad-turbocharged W16 engine producing 1,600 PS (1,176 kW / 1,600 hp), delivering 100 PS more than the standard Chiron.

Performance Focus:
Engineered primarily for extreme straight-line (longitudinal) speed, emphasizing drag reduction and aerodynamic stability.

Aerodynamics & Engineering

Aerodynamic Design:
Features a distinctive longtail rear extension to ensure smooth laminar airflow and significantly reduced drag at very high speeds.

Stability Enhancements:
Incorporates air curtains, optimized airflow channels, and a newly developed rear diffuser for enhanced stability at extreme velocities.

Design & Styling

Exterior Design:
Easily recognizable by its exposed jet-black carbon fiber body finished with Jet Orange racing stripes.

Brand Details:
Accented with matching Jet Orange interior highlights and a silver-and-black Bugatti emblem.

Interior & Cabin

Interior Theme:
A luxurious, performance-focused cockpit crafted with carbon fiber and Alcantara, inspired by a fighter-jet aesthetic.

Infotainment:
Designed without a central infotainment screen to maintain a pure, driver-focused environment.
